Upcycle LATVIA

Sagatavoja: Roberts Deglis

Rēzeknes novads, 23.08.2016.


  • One Latvian produces approx. 310 kg of waste a year, which means that a 3-people-family produces almost a ton of waste per year. In Latvia we produce approx. 700 000 tonnes of waste every year.
  • Unfortunately, only 15% of all waste in Latvia are recycled but the rest 90% are stored in waste sites.

Recycling

  • One Latvian produces about 78 kg of wastepaper a year, which is the same amount of paper that can be produced from one tree.
  • Recycling a ton of wastepaper you can get up to 800 kg of usable paper. At paper mill «Līgatne» around 35 tonnes of wastepaper are recycled every day, 13 000 tonnes every year.
